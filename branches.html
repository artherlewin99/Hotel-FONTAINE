BRANCHES





Hikkaduwa

An unbeatable sanctuary for nature lovers and beach lovers alike, Hikkaduwa's coastal hotel branch is renowned for its mesmerising coral reefs and immaculate sandy shoreline.




Unawatuna

The Unawatuna location of Hotel Fontaine is famed for its magnificent beach, where the Indian Ocean's crystal-clear waves and golden beaches combine to create a romantic seaside getaway.



Arugambay

The Arugambay location of Hotel Fontaine is a surfers' dream come true, featuring not only world-class waves but also gorgeous beaches and mouthwatering seafood, making it a seaside sanctuary for the daring gourmand.
