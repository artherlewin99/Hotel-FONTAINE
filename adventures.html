ADVENTURES



Surfing


Indulge in an amazing surfing experience at Hotel Fontaine, where you can ride the waves of adventure while being guided by a pro and paint priceless memories on the ocean's canvas. Our surf programmes are suitable for all skill levels, whether you are an experienced surfer or a beginner, guaranteeing a fun and secure ride.




Diving


Take advantage of the top-notch diving opportunities offered by Hotel Fontaine to explore colourful coral reefs and marvel at fascinating marine life in the clean waters of the Indian Ocean. Divers of all experience levels will have a safe and exciting underwater journey thanks to our qualified instructors and first-rate facilities.
