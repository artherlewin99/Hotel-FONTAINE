UNLIMITED SUNDAY...!








WHAT IS UNLIMITED SUNDAY...?

With our "Unlimited Sunday" event, indulge in a culinary adventure every Sunday at Hotel FONTANE's restaurant, "Salt & Pepper. Enjoy a lavish buffet feast where you may eat as much as you want for only 2000 rupees.




WHAT YOU CAN EAT...?

Enjoy the powerful and aromatic tastes of Sri Lankan, Chinese, and Italian cuisines at Hotel FONTANE's "Salt & Pepper" during "Unlimited Sunday," along with a delectable selection of light nibbles and refreshing smoothies. It's a culinary adventure that promises a wide range of flavours to satisfy all palates.
